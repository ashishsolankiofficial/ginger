Welcome to Premium Delivery

Instructions:
  • Go to Restaurants page from the page header.
  • One can View/Edit Restaurant details by clicking on restaurant name or image.
  • To create an order for particular restaurant click on attach orders.
  • One can filter products on product selection page.
  • Choose quantity & add products to Cart.
  • Go to Cart page from page header & click on place order to create an Order.
  • To view all the past orders click on Orders from page header.
