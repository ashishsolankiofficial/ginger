Welcome to Premium Delivery

Premium Delivery is a B2B Grocery supplier which supplies top grocery products to restaurants all over India. You have logged in as a manager/sales for this company. With this platform you can create grocery order for restaurants.

Instructions:
  • Go to Restaurants page from the page header.
  • One can View/Edit Restaurant details by clicking on restaurant name or image.
  • To create an order for particular restaurant click on attach orders.
  • One can filter products on product selection page.
  • Choose quantity & add products to Cart.
  • Go to Cart page from page header & click on place order to create an Order.
  • To view all the past orders click on Orders from page header.
Note:
  • Images on product selection page can take upto 10 seconds to load as they are not cached in backend server.
  • Some Images on product selection page might not load, as they is sourced from external source.
